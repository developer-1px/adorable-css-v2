Why we built this.

We reimagined CSS to be cleaner for humans and unambiguous for machines. It's not just a utility library; it's a language.

Hallucination-Free AI

LLMs struggle with names like flex-row-reverse. AdorableCSS uses semantic, predictable tokens like hbox(reverse) that AI guesses right 99% of the time.
